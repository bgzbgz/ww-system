14 Fast Track signature tool writing guide

**Prompt for Creating World-Class Fast Track Tool Instructions (with Case Study)**

Below is the tailored prompt for designing a tool instruction document using ChatGPT, now including a requirement for a second case study to demonstrate successful implementation. This enhances credibility and provides practical, relatable examples.

-----
**Prompt**:

*"You are tasked with creating a world-class instruction guide for a Fast Track signature tool. Follow the principles and identity of the Fast Track methodology to ensure the document is simple, impactful, and actionable. The tool must inspire confidence, highlight its scientific foundation, and provide clear, step-by-step instructions for immediate use. The guide should align with the Fast Track style, prioritizing directness, practicality, and results-driven clarity. Here are the elements to include:"*

-----
**Structure of the Document**

1. **Title and Tagline**:
   1. Name the tool (e.g., *The Prioritization Matrix*) and add a Fast Track-branded tagline emphasizing its benefit (e.g., *“Fast Track Prioritization Matrix: Focus on What Drives Results”*).
1. **Purpose and Benefit**:
   1. Write a short section explaining:
      1. *What the tool is*: Describe it in one or two simple sentences.
      1. *Why it matters*: Highlight the benefit and how it helps achieve measurable success.
1. **Scientific and Practical Credibility**:
   1. Provide a brief explanation of the tool’s foundation (e.g., decision science, behavioral psychology, or industry-proven frameworks).
   1. Include **two short real-world case studies** demonstrating successful implementation:
      1. **Case Study 1**: Highlight a tangible, measurable impact the tool has had for a company or team.
      1. **Case Study 2**: Show how the tool solved a different problem or was adapted to another scenario.
1. **Visual Explanation**:
   1. Incorporate a diagram, chart, or step-by-step visual that makes the tool easy to understand at a glance.
   1. Include a blank or example template that users can follow.
1. **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
   1. Provide clear, numbered steps for using the tool, ensuring simplicity and brevity.
   1. Add estimated time for each step if applicable.
1. **Common Mistakes to Avoid**:
   1. List 3-5 common mistakes users may make and how to avoid them.
   1. Provide quick examples of what *not* to do and why.
1. **Checklist for Success**:
   1. Include a checklist to ensure the tool is used effectively.
   1. Example:
      1. Have you identified the right inputs?
      1. Are the results validated with relevant stakeholders?
1. **Practical Application and Adaptability**:
   1. Suggest ways to adapt the tool for different industries or scenarios.
   1. Highlight any flexibility in usage.
1. **Closing Section**:
   1. End with an inspiring message that reinforces the value of the tool and its ability to drive results.
   1. Example: *“With this tool, you’ll cut through the noise, focus on what matters, and drive meaningful progress faster than ever.”*
1. **Additional Resources**:
   1. Offer links or references to templates, FAQs, or additional reading for users who want to dive deeper.
-----
**Guidelines for Style and Tone**

- **Direct and Clear**:
  - Use simple language and short sentences. Avoid jargon or unnecessary complexity.
- **Bold and Honest**:
  - Use strong, confident statements. Be brutally honest about what the tool can and cannot do.
- **Action-Oriented**:
  - Focus on steps, outcomes, and immediate applicability.
- **Visually Engaging**:
  - Include icons, diagrams, or templates for quick comprehension.
- **Customer-Centric**:
  - Emphasize the benefits to the user and their specific challenges.
-----
**Example Use of Prompt with Case Studies**

**Input to ChatGPT**:\
*"The tool is a prioritization matrix designed to help leaders rank customer pains, needs, and gains based on severity, frequency, and strategic impact. The matrix is based on decision science and aims to focus teams on the highest-value actions. Create the instruction guide using the structure above. Include two case studies: (1) A B2B SaaS company reducing feature bloat, and (2) A retail brand optimizing customer support efforts."*

**Output from ChatGPT**:\
The output would include a polished, structured guide with:

- A clear title and purpose.
- Two practical case studies (e.g., the SaaS company prioritizing features based on customer data, and the retail brand reducing customer wait times by addressing the most frequent complaints).
- Visual templates for the prioritization matrix.
- Step-by-step instructions, common mistakes, and tips for success.
